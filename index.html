Confidential Resources for Teens in Solano County

Your Rights as a Teen in California

As a teen in California, you should know that state law protects your right to confidential STI testing. If you're 12 or older, you can get tested and treated without your parents' permission, and healthcare providers are not allowed to tell your parents.
